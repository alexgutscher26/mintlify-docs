## Welcome to Your ReplyIQ Dashboard

The ReplyIQ dashboard is your central hub for managing AI-powered social media engagement. From here, you can configure AI models, track usage analytics, manage team members, and monitor your subscription.

- **Latest Generations**: Recent reply generations with timestamps
- **Model Usage**: Breakdown of AI models used
- **Platform Distribution**: Usage across different social platforms
