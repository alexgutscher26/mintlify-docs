## Main Sections

### 1. Navigation Sidebar

The sidebar provides quick access to all dashboard sections:

- **Home**: Overview of your account and recent activity
- **AI Tools**: Access to all content generation tools
  - Hashtag Generator
  - Image Caption Generator
  - Language Translator
  - Sentiment Analysis
  - Story Generator
  - Thread Generator
  - Video Script Generator
  - Brand Voice Training
- **Analytics**: Usage statistics and performance metrics
- **Settings**: Account, billing, and application settings
- **Support**: Access help resources and contact support

### 2. Home Dashboard

The home dashboard provides a quick overview of:

- **Recent Activity**: Your latest generated content
- **Usage Summary**: Current usage metrics and limits
- **Quick Access**: Shortcuts to your most-used tools
- **Updates**: Latest platform news and feature announcements

### 3. AI Tools Section

Each AI tool has its own dedicated interface with:

- **Input Area**: Where you enter your content or parameters
- **Configuration Options**: Settings to customize the output
- **Output Display**: Where generated content appears
- **Action Buttons**: Copy, save, edit, or share your content

### 4. Analytics Section

Track your usage and performance with:

- **Usage Metrics**: See how much you've used each tool
- **Usage Trends**: View usage patterns over time
- **Subscription Status**: Monitor your current plan and limits

### 5. Settings Section

Manage your account and preferences:

- **Profile Settings**: Update your personal information
- **API Configuration**: Set up and manage your AI provider API keys
- **Subscription Management**: View, upgrade, or change your subscription
- **Brand Voice Training**: Configure your brand's unique voice for AI generation
- **Notification Preferences**: Control email and in-app notifications

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Profile">
    Complete your profile information in the Settings section to personalize your experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure AI Providers">
    Add your API keys for OpenAI, Mistral, or Google AI in the Settings → General → AI Model Provider section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train Your Brand Voice">
    Use the Brand Voice Training tool to customize AI outputs to match your brand's tone and style.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Your First Content">
    Select any tool from the sidebar and follow the prompts to create your first piece of AI-generated content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Effective Use

- **Save Templates**: Create and save templates for frequently used content types
- **Explore Tool Settings**: Each tool has advanced settings that can significantly improve results
- **Review Analytics**: Regularly check your usage patterns to optimize your subscription
- **Update API Keys**: Keep your AI provider API keys up to date for uninterrupted service
- **Use Keyboard Shortcuts**: Learn the keyboard shortcuts to speed up your workflow
