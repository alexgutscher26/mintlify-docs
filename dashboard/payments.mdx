# Payments & Billing

The Payments & Billing section allows you to manage your ReplyIQ subscription, update payment methods, view invoices, and track your billing history.

## Subscription Management

<Frame>
  ![Subscription Management](/images/dashboard/subscription.png)
  <Caption>Manage your ReplyIQ subscription plan and billing cycle</Caption>
</Frame>

### Current Plan

Your current subscription plan is displayed at the top of the Payments page, showing:

- **Plan Name**: Free, Pro, Business, or Enterprise
- **Billing Cycle**: Monthly or Annual
- **Next Billing Date**: When your next payment will be processed
- **Plan Status**: Active, Trial, or Canceled

### Change Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Plan Options">
    Click the "Change Plan" button next to your current plan details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compare Plans">
    Review the available plans and their features:
    
    <CardGroup cols={4}>
      <Card title="Free" icon="gift">
        - 50 generations/month
        - Basic tools only
        - Standard support
      </Card>
      <Card title="Pro" icon="user">
        - 500 generations/month
        - All tools
        - Priority support
      </Card>
      <Card title="Business" icon="building">
        - 2,000 generations/month
        - All tools
        - Team collaboration
        - Advanced analytics
      </Card>
      <Card title="Enterprise" icon="city">
        - Custom limits
        - All tools
        - Dedicated support
        - Custom integrations
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    Choose your desired plan and billing cycle (monthly or annual)
    
    <Tip>
      Annual billing offers a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Change">
    Review the prorated charges or credits and confirm your plan change
    
    <Warning>
      Downgrading your plan may result in immediate loss of access to certain features.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Cancel Subscription

To cancel your subscription:

1. Click the "Cancel Subscription" button
2. Select your reason for cancellation from the dropdown menu
3. Provide any additional feedback (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

<Warning>
  Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll maintain access to your current plan until then.
</Warning>

## Payment Methods

Manage the payment methods associated with your account:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="View Payment Methods">
    Your current payment methods are listed with:
    - Last 4 digits of the card
    - Card type (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)
    - Expiration date
    - Billing address
    - Default status
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Add Payment Method">
    To add a new payment method:
    
    1. Click "Add Payment Method"
    2. Enter your card details
    3. Add billing address
    4. Set as default (optional)
    5. Save
    
    <Info>
      ReplyIQ accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Edit Payment Method">
    You can update the following for existing payment methods:
    - Expiration date
    - Billing address
    - Default status
    
    <Warning>
      To change the card number, you must add a new payment method.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Remove Payment Method">
    To remove a payment method:
    
    1. Click the "Remove" button next to the payment method
    2. Confirm deletion
    
    <Warning>
      You cannot remove your default payment method if you have an active subscription.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing History

View and manage your past invoices and payment history:

<Frame>
  ![Billing History](/images/dashboard/billing-history.png)
  <Caption>Track your payment history and download invoices</Caption>
</Frame>

The billing history table shows:

- **Invoice Date**: When the invoice was issued
- **Billing Period**: The service period covered by the invoice
- **Amount**: Total amount charged
- **Status**: Paid, Pending, Failed, or Refunded
- **Actions**: Download PDF invoice or receipt

<Tip>
  You can filter your billing history by date range, amount, or status.
</Tip>

### Download Invoices

To download an invoice:

1. Locate the invoice in your billing history
2. Click the "Download" button in the Actions column
3. Select PDF or CSV format

### Tax Information

To add or update your tax information:

1. Click "Tax Information" in the sidebar
2. Enter your business name, address, and tax ID
3. Save changes

<Info>
  For business accounts, adding valid tax information may exempt you from certain taxes depending on your jurisdiction.
</Info>

## Payment Issues

If there's an issue with your payment:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Failed Payments">
    If a payment fails, you'll receive:
    - In-app notification
    - Email alert
    - Grace period to update payment information
    
    <Warning>
      After the grace period (typically 7 days), your account access may be limited until payment is resolved.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Resolve Payment Issues">
    To resolve payment issues:
    
    1. Check your payment method details
    2. Ensure sufficient funds are available
    3. Update payment information if needed
    4. Click "Retry Payment"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Refund Requests">
    To request a refund:
    
    1. Contact [billing@replyiq.com](mailto:billing@replyiq.com)
    2. Include your account email and invoice number
    3. Explain your refund reason
    
    <Info>
      Refund eligibility is determined by our [Refund Policy](/legal/refund-policy).
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing Notifications

Manage how you receive billing notifications:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Notification Settings">
    Click "Notification Preferences" in the billing section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Alerts">
    Toggle notifications for:
    - Payment receipts
    - Upcoming renewals
    - Payment failures
    - Plan changes
    - Usage thresholds
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Delivery Methods">
    Choose how to receive notifications:
    - Email
    - In-app
    - SMS (for critical alerts)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enterprise Billing

Enterprise customers have additional billing options:

- **Purchase Orders**: Submit and track purchase orders
- **Net Terms**: Pay invoices on net-30, net-60, or custom terms
- **Volume Discounts**: Automatic discounts based on usage volume
- **Custom Invoicing**: Receive invoices tailored to your accounting requirements

<Info>
  To set up enterprise billing, contact your account manager or [enterprise@replyiq.com](mailto:enterprise@replyiq.com).
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When am I charged?">
    For monthly subscriptions, you're charged on the same date each month. For annual subscriptions, you're charged once per year on your subscription anniversary.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I get a receipt for tax purposes?">
    You can download tax receipts for any payment from your billing history. These receipts contain all information needed for tax filing.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my plan limits?">
    If you reach your generation limit, you'll be notified and given options to:
    1. Upgrade to a higher tier plan
    2. Purchase additional one-time credits
    3. Wait until your next billing cycle when limits reset
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Refund eligibility depends on several factors. Please review our [Refund Policy](/legal/refund-policy) or contact [billing@replyiq.com](mailto:billing@replyiq.com) for assistance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
