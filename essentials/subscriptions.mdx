## Managing Your Subscription

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="credit-card" title="Upgrade to Pro">
    1. Navigate to Dashboard Settings
    2. Click on 'Subscription'
    3. Select 'Upgrade to Pro'
    4. Enter payment information
    5. Confirm subscription

    Your Pro features will be activated immediately after successful payment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="gear" title="Update Billing Information">
    To update your payment method or billing details:
    1. Go to Dashboard Settings
    2. Select 'Billing'
    3. Click 'Update Payment Method'
    4. Enter new card information
    5. Save changes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="calendar" title="Billing Cycles">
    - Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually
    - Monthly plans are charged on the same day each month
    - Annual plans offer a 20% discount
    - Automatic renewal unless cancelled
    - Pro-rated charges for mid-cycle upgrades
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="question" title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    To cancel your Pro subscription:
    1. Go to Dashboard Settings
    2. Navigate to 'Subscription'
    3. Click 'Cancel Subscription'
    4. Confirm cancellation

    Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="arrows-rotate" title="Can I switch between plans?">
    Yes, you can switch between plans at any time:
    - Upgrading: Immediate access to Pro features
    - Downgrading: Changes take effect at the end of billing cycle
    - Pro-rated charges apply for upgrades
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="receipt" title="Billing & Invoices">
    - Access all invoices in Dashboard Settings
    - Download PDF invoices for accounting
    - View payment history
    - Set custom billing email
    - Add VAT/tax information
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
