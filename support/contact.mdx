## What to Include in Your Support Request

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include the following information in your support request:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Account Information">
    - Your account email address
    - Your name
    - Your subscription plan (Free, Pro, Enterprise)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Issue Details">
    - Clear description of the issue
    - Steps to reproduce the problem
    - When the issue started occurring
    - Any error messages you received
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="System Information">
    - Browser and version
    - Operating system
    - Device type (desktop, mobile)
    - Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Support Hours

Our support team is available during the following hours:

- **Monday - Friday**: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST
- **Saturday - Sunday**: Limited support for urgent issues

## Priority Support

Enterprise customers receive priority support with faster response times and dedicated support representatives. If you're interested in upgrading to an Enterprise plan, please contact our [sales team](mailto:sales@replyiq.com).

## Feature Requests

Have an idea for improving ReplyIQ? We'd love to hear it! Send your feature requests to [feedback@replyiq.com](mailto:feedback@replyiq.com) with the subject line "Feature Request: [Your Idea]".

## Security Issues

If you've discovered a security vulnerability, please report it immediately to [security@replyiq.com](mailto:security@replyiq.com). We take security issues very seriously and will respond promptly.

## Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve ReplyIQ. Share your thoughts, suggestions, or general feedback with us at [feedback@replyiq.com](mailto:feedback@replyiq.com).
