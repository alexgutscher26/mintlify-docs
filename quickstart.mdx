## Overview

This quickstart guide will help you set up ReplyIQ and generate your first AI-powered social media reply in under 5 minutes.

<Info>
  **Prerequisites**: You'll need a modern web browser. <b>API keys for AI services (OpenAI, Claude, Google, etc.) are required to generate replies.</b> See below for details on obtaining these keys.
</Info>

<Warning>
  <b>Security Note:</b> Never share your API keys publicly. Treat them as sensitive credentials.
</Warning>

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [replyiq.com](https://replyiq.com) and create your account using:
    - Email and password
    - Google OAuth
    - GitHub OAuth
    - Discord OAuth
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your email and click the verification link to activate your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Fill in your profile information and select your use case (individual, team, business)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Configure AI Services

<Warning>
  AI service configuration is required to generate replies.<br/>
  <b>Important:</b> The first user to sign up automatically becomes an admin. Make sure this is a trusted account.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Admin Dashboard">
    Navigate to **Dashboard â†’ Settings â†’ AI Model Provider**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add API Keys">
    Configure your preferred AI services. <b>You'll need to obtain API keys from each provider's website:</b>
    - <b>OpenAI:</b> [https://platform.openai.com/account/api-keys](https://platform.openai.com/account/api-keys)
    - <b>Anthropic (Claude):</b> [https://console.anthropic.com/settings/keys](https://console.anthropic.com/settings/keys)
    - <b>Google AI (Gemini):</b> [https://makersuite.google.com/app/apikey](https://makersuite.google.com/app/apikey)
    
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="OpenAI">
        ```json
        {
          "provider": "openai",
          "apiKey": "sk-...",
          "models": ["gpt-4", "gpt-3.5-turbo"]
        }
        ```
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Claude">
        ```json
        {
          "provider": "anthropic",
          "apiKey": "sk-ant-...",
          "models": ["claude-3-opus", "claude-3-sonnet"]
        }
        ```
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Google AI">
        ```json
        {
          "provider": "google",
          "apiKey": "AIza...",
          "models": ["gemini-pro", "gemini-pro-vision"]
        }
        ```
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Connection">
    Click **Test Connection** to verify your API keys are working correctly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Configuration">
    Save your settings and set default models for different use cases
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Install Browser Extension

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Chrome" icon="chrome">
    1. Visit [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore)
    2. Search for "ReplyIQ"
    3. Click "Add to Chrome"
    4. Pin the extension to your toolbar
    5. <b>Note:</b> The extension may not be available in all regions. Check for updates after installation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Firefox" icon="firefox">
    1. Visit [Firefox Add-ons](https://addons.mozilla.org)
    2. Search for "ReplyIQ"
    3. Click "Add to Firefox"
    4. Grant necessary permissions
    5. <b>Note:</b> The extension may not be available in all regions. Check for updates after installation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edge" icon="edge">
    1. Visit [Edge Add-ons](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons)
    2. Search for "ReplyIQ"
    3. Click "Get"
    4. Enable the extension
    5. <b>Note:</b> The extension may not be available in all regions. Check for updates after installation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Extension Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Click the ReplyIQ extension icon and sign in with your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant Permissions">
    Allow the extension to access social media sites when prompted
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    The extension should show "Connected" status with your account info
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Generate Your First Reply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Social Media">
    Go to Twitter/X, LinkedIn, or Facebook and find a post you want to reply to
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate ReplyIQ">
    - Click the reply button on the post
    - Look for the ReplyIQ icon in the text area
    - Or use the keyboard shortcut: `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Reply">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Tone Selection">
        Choose from available tones:
        - **Professional**: Formal, business-appropriate
        - **Casual**: Relaxed, conversational
        - **Friendly**: Warm, approachable
        - **Humorous**: Light-hearted, witty
        - **Supportive**: Encouraging, empathetic
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Platform Optimization">
        ReplyIQ automatically optimizes for the current platform:
        - **Twitter/X**: Character limits, hashtag suggestions
        - **LinkedIn**: Professional tone, industry context
        - **Facebook**: Conversational, engagement-focused
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Reply">
    Click **Generate Reply** and watch as AI creates a contextual response
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Edit">
    - Review the generated reply
    - Make any necessary edits
    - Add emojis using the smart suggestions
    - Click **Use Reply** to insert it into the text field
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Usage Tips

<Tip>
  **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Use `Ctrl+Shift+R` (or `Cmd+Shift+R` on Mac) to quickly activate ReplyIQ on any social media platform.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Tone Consistency**: The extension remembers your last used tone for consistent brand voice across replies.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Context Matters**: The AI analyzes the original post content, so more context leads to better replies.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Extension not appearing">
    1. Refresh the social media page
    2. Check if the extension is enabled in your browser
    3. Verify you're signed in to your ReplyIQ account
    4. Try disabling and re-enabling the extension
    5. Clear your browser cache and cookies
    6. <b>Still stuck?</b> Visit our [FAQ](/support/faq) or [contact support](mailto:support@replyiq.com)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="AI generation failing">
    1. Check your AI service configuration in the dashboard
    2. Verify your API keys are valid and have sufficient credits
    3. Try switching to a different AI model
    4. Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Slow response times">
    1. Check your internet connection
    2. Try using a different AI model (some are faster than others)
    3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
    4. Restart your browser
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
