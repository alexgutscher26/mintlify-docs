# Browser Extension Installation

The ReplyIQ browser extension brings AI-powered content generation directly to your favorite social media platforms. This guide will walk you through the installation and setup process for different browsers.

## Supported Browsers

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Chrome" icon="chrome">
    Chrome 88+ and Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Opera)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Firefox" icon="firefox">
    Firefox 78+
  </Card>
  <Card title="Safari" icon="safari">
    Safari 14+ (macOS only)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## System Requirements

- **Windows**: Windows 10 or later
- **macOS**: macOS 10.14 (Mojave) or later
- **Linux**: Ubuntu 18.04+, Fedora 30+, or other modern distributions
- **Mobile**: Not currently supported (desktop browsers only)

## Installation Instructions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Chrome">
    ### Chrome Installation
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit Chrome Web Store">
        Go to the [ReplyIQ Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/replyiq) page in the Chrome Web Store.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Add to Chrome">
        Click the **Add to Chrome** button in the upper right corner.
        
        <Frame>
          ![Add to Chrome](/images/extension/chrome-add.png)
          <Caption>Click "Add to Chrome" to begin installation</Caption>
        </Frame>
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Confirm Installation">
        A popup will appear asking you to confirm the installation. Click **Add extension**.
        
        <Warning>
          Make sure you're installing from the official Chrome Web Store to avoid malicious extensions.
        </Warning>
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Pin the Extension">
        After installation, the ReplyIQ icon will appear in your browser's extension area. Click the puzzle piece icon, find ReplyIQ, and click the pin icon to keep it visible in your toolbar.
        
        <Frame>
          ![Pin Extension](/images/extension/chrome-pin.png)
          <Caption>Pin the extension for easy access</Caption>
        </Frame>
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Firefox">
    ### Firefox Installation
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit Firefox Add-ons">
        Go to the [ReplyIQ Firefox Add-on](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/replyiq) page.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Add to Firefox">
        Click the **Add to Firefox** button.
        
        <Frame>
          ![Add to Firefox](/images/extension/firefox-add.png)
          <Caption>Click "Add to Firefox" to begin installation</Caption>
        </Frame>
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Confirm Permissions">
        Review the permissions requested by the extension and click **Add** when prompted.
        
        <Info>
          ReplyIQ requires certain permissions to function properly, including access to social media sites and your ReplyIQ account.
        </Info>
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Access the Extension">
        After installation, the ReplyIQ icon will appear in your browser's toolbar. If you don't see it, click the menu button (three lines) and select **Add-ons and themes** to manage your extensions.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Safari">
    ### Safari Installation
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit App Store">
        Go to the [Mac App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/replyiq) and search for "ReplyIQ for Safari".
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Download the Extension">
        Click the **Get** button to download and install the extension.
        
        <Warning>
          Safari extensions are distributed as Mac apps through the App Store. You'll need to install both the app and enable the extension.
        </Warning>
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Enable in Safari">
        1. Open Safari
        2. Go to **Safari → Preferences → Extensions**
        3. Find ReplyIQ in the list and check the box to enable it
        
        <Frame>
          ![Enable in Safari](/images/extension/safari-enable.png)
          <Caption>Enable the ReplyIQ extension in Safari preferences</Caption>
        </Frame>
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Confirm Permissions">
        Safari may ask you to confirm permissions for the extension. Click **Allow** to grant the necessary permissions.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Edge">
    ### Microsoft Edge Installation
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit Edge Add-ons">
        Go to the [Microsoft Edge Add-ons](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons) page and search for "ReplyIQ".
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Get the Extension">
        Click the **Get** button next to the ReplyIQ extension.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Add Extension">
        In the popup dialog, click **Add extension** to confirm.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Pin to Toolbar">
        Click the extensions icon (puzzle piece) in the toolbar, find ReplyIQ, and click the pin icon to keep it visible.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Info>
      Since Edge is Chromium-based, you can also install the extension from the Chrome Web Store by enabling "Allow extensions from other stores" in Edge's extension settings.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Post-Installation Setup

After installing the extension, you'll need to connect it to your ReplyIQ account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Extension">
    Click the ReplyIQ icon in your browser toolbar to open the extension popup.
    
    <Frame>
      ![Extension Popup](/images/extension/extension-popup.png)
      <Caption>The extension popup before signing in</Caption>
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Click the **Sign In** button and enter your ReplyIQ credentials.
    
    <Tip>
      If you're already signed in to ReplyIQ in another browser tab, the extension may authenticate automatically.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Grant Permissions">
    The extension will request permission to access your ReplyIQ account. Click **Allow** to proceed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Preferences">
    After signing in, you can configure your extension preferences:
    
    - **Default AI Model**: Choose your preferred AI model for content generation
    - **Default Tone**: Set your preferred tone for generated content
    - **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Customize keyboard shortcuts for quick access
    - **Notification Settings**: Control when and how the extension notifies you
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Extension Not Appearing">
    If the ReplyIQ icon or integration doesn't appear:
    
    1. Refresh the page
    2. Verify the extension is enabled in your browser's extension settings
    3. Check if you're signed in to your ReplyIQ account
    4. Ensure you're on a supported platform and page
    5. Try disabling and re-enabling the extension
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Authentication Issues">
    If you're having trouble signing in:
    
    1. Clear your browser cookies for replyiq.com
    2. Sign in to the ReplyIQ web app first, then try the extension
    3. Check if your account subscription is active
    4. Try signing out and signing back in
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Content Generation Failing">
    If content generation isn't working:
    
    1. Verify your API configuration in the ReplyIQ dashboard
    2. Check your internet connection
    3. Ensure you haven't reached your usage limits
    4. Try a different AI model
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Browser Compatibility">
    If you're experiencing browser-specific issues:
    
    1. Update your browser to the latest version
    2. Disable conflicting extensions temporarily
    3. Try in incognito/private mode to check for extension conflicts
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Updating the Extension

The ReplyIQ extension updates automatically in most browsers. To check for updates manually:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Chrome">
    1. Go to `chrome://extensions`
    2. Enable "Developer mode" in the top right
    3. Click "Update" button in the top left
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Firefox">
    1. Go to `about:addons`
    2. Click the gear icon and select "Check for Updates"
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Safari">
    1. Open the App Store
    2. Go to the Updates tab
    3. Look for ReplyIQ in the list of available updates
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Edge">
    1. Go to `edge://extensions`
    2. Click "Updates" in the left sidebar
    3. Click "Update all" or update ReplyIQ individually
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Uninstalling the Extension

If you need to uninstall the extension:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Chrome">
    1. Right-click the ReplyIQ icon in the toolbar
    2. Select "Remove from Chrome..."
    3. Confirm removal when prompted
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Firefox">
    1. Go to `about:addons`
    2. Find ReplyIQ in your extensions list
    3. Click the three dots next to it and select "Remove"
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Safari">
    1. Go to Safari → Preferences → Extensions
    2. Uncheck the box next to ReplyIQ to disable it
    3. To completely remove it, uninstall the app from your Applications folder
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Edge">
    1. Right-click the ReplyIQ icon in the toolbar
    2. Select "Remove from Microsoft Edge"
    3. Confirm removal when prompted
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
