## Overview

ReplyIQ's Multi-Platform Support enables you to generate AI-powered replies tailored for each major social media platform. Whether you're engaging on Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, or Instagram, ReplyIQ adapts its responses to fit the unique culture, character limits, and best practices of each platform.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Platform Detection">
    ReplyIQ automatically detects the platform you're using via the browser extension or API request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Context Analysis">
    The AI analyzes the original post, platform conventions, and your selected tone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reply Generation">
    Generates a reply optimized for the detected platform, including character limits, hashtags, and tone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="User Review">
    Preview, edit, and post your reply directly from the extension or dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Platform Not Detected">

    - Refresh the page or restart your browser
    - Check extension permissions for the site
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Formatting Issues">
    - Some platforms may render emojis or formatting differently
    - Edit replies before posting to ensure correct appearance
  </Accordion>
