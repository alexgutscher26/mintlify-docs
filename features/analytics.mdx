## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Data Collection">
    ReplyIQ automatically tracks all relevant activity—reply generations, API calls, team actions, and engagement events.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dashboard Visualization">
    Data is visualized in your dashboard with charts, tables, and key metrics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Custom Analysis">
    Filter, sort, and export data to analyze trends and optimize your strategy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continuous Improvement">
    Use analytics insights to refine your AI settings, team workflows, and engagement tactics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Usage Tips & Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Monitor trends:** Regularly review your analytics dashboard to spot usage spikes, engagement drops, or model performance changes.
</Tip>
<Tip>
  **Set alerts:** Use notifications to avoid hitting usage or credit limits unexpectedly.
</Tip>
<Tip>
  **Compare models:** Use model usage and performance data to choose the best AI for your needs.
</Tip>
<Tip>
  **Share insights:** Export reports to share results with your team or stakeholders.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Data Not Updating">
    - Refresh your dashboard or log out and back in
    - Check your internet connection
    - Wait a few minutes for data to sync
    - Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Missing Metrics">
    - Ensure you have the correct permissions to view team or advanced analytics
    - Check your plan for feature availability
    - Contact support for help with custom reports
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Export Issues">
    - Make sure pop-ups are allowed for downloads
    - Try exporting in a different format (CSV or PDF)
    - Contact support if exports fail repeatedly
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
