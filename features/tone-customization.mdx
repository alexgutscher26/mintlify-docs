## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Tone">
    Choose your desired tone in the extension interface or dashboard before generating a reply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Generation">
    The AI uses your selected tone to craft a reply that matches your intent and platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Edit">
    Preview the generated reply, make edits if needed, and ensure the tone fits your brand and context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Defaults (Optional)">
    Set a default tone in your preferences for consistent voice across all replies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Usage Tips & Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Match the platform:** Use professional tone for LinkedIn, casual or friendly for Facebook, and playful or humorous for Twitter/Instagram.
</Tip>
<Tip>
  **Stay consistent:** Stick to one tone per conversation thread to maintain a coherent voice.
</Tip>
<Tip>
  **Review before posting:** Always check that the tone matches your intent and audience.
</Tip>
<Tip>
  **Experiment:** Try different tones to see what resonates best with your audience and brand.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't Change Tone">
    - Make sure you're using the latest version of the extension or web app
    - Check that tone selection is enabled in your settings
    - Refresh the page or restart the extension if needed
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tone Doesn't Match Output">
    - Provide more context in your reply or original post
    - Try a different tone or adjust your instructions
    - Edit the generated reply to better fit your intent
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Default Tone Not Saving">
    - Ensure you have saved your preferences in the dashboard or extension
    - Check for extension or app updates
    - Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
